# Multi-Perspective Macroeconomic Policy Impact Analysis

## Overview
This skill provides a structured framework for analyzing the impact of macroeconomic policies on specific companies or industries, using four distinct professional perspectives (Economist, Historian, Sociologist, Entrepreneur) to derive comprehensive investment or strategic insights.

## Trigger Conditions
- User requests analysis of a company/industry in relation to macroeconomic policies
- User mentions "macroeconomic research report" or similar frameworks
- User asks for multi-perspective analysis of policy impacts
- User provides policy news (government, ministry, diplomatic) and asks for company impact assessment
- **User selects A/B/C option**:
  - **Option A**: Data-driven single optimal policy recommendation (search inflation data → tariff contribution → optimal path)
  - **Option B**: Policy essence discussion first, then evaluate response
  - **Option C**: Logical framework without specific data

## Critical Constraints (Always Apply)
- ❌ **NO investment rankings or operation recommendations**
- ❌ **NO position/sizing recommendations**
- ✅ **Focus on**: Policy direction analysis, market impact (short/mid/long term), personal development opportunities (asset appreciation, entrepreneurship)
- ✅ **Time zone**: Beijing Time (UTC+8) for all task scheduling and news dates

## Step-by-Step Process

### 1. Data Collection Phase
**Search for recent policy news:**
- **Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (MIIT)**: Search "工信部 + [timeframe: 近两周] + 宏观经济/产业政策"
- **Ministry of Finance (MOF)**: Search "财政部 + [timeframe: 近两周] + 宏观经济/财政政策"
- **Diplomatic visits**: Search "外交 + [timeframe: 近两月] + 访华/外国领导人"
- **Foreign enterprise visits**: Search "外企 + [timeframe: 近两月] + 访华/投资/合作"

**Search for company-specific news:**
- Company name + "2026年 + [months] + 新闻/业绩/战略"
- Company name + "Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4 + 财报/营收/净利润"
- Company name + "AI/大模型/即时零售/下沉市场/战略调整"
- Company name + "竞争格局/市场份额/抖音/阿里/京东" (competitor analysis)

### 3. Analysis Framework (Choose Based on User Option)

#### **Option A: First Principles Data-Driven Approach** (Recommended for A option)
**Logic Chain**: Inflation Type → Policy Priority → Optimal Rate Path

**Step 1: Data Decoding** (Must find specific numbers)
- Search: Tariff contribution to CPI (%), PCE structure decomposition
- Search: Fed dot plot (2026 rate cuts/幅度), Powell's latest讲话
- Search: Trump tariff measures (rate/scope/effective date), impact on Chinese exports (%)

**Step 2: Root Cause Analysis**
- **Question**: Is current inflation supply shock (tariffs) or demand-pull?
- **Evidence**: Tariff contribution rate + PCE structure
- **Conclusion**: [Explicit type, e.g., "70% tariff supply shock, 30% demand-pull"]

**Step 3: Policy Priority**
- **Question**: What is Fed's primary goal now?
- **Evidence**: Dot plot + employment data + inflation expectations
- **Conclusion**: [Explicit priority, e.g., "Inflation control > Employment protection"]

**Step 4: Optimal Rate Path Derivation**
- **Logic**: Inflation type → Policy priority → Rate decision
- **Prohibited**: Do NOT assume "high inflation = high rates"
- **Conclusion**: [Single optimal path, e.g., "Maintain 4.5%-4.75% until Q2 2026, cut 25bp in Q3"]

#### **Option B: Policy Essence Discussion**
- Discuss tariff policy fundamentals first
- Then evaluate Fed's likely response
- Provide qualitative framework

#### **Option C: Logical Framework Only**
- Provide decision-critical logic structure
- No specific data required
- Focus on reasoning chain

#### **四视角分析 (Optional, for depth)**
*Only apply if user explicitly requests multi-perspective depth after Option A/B/C analysis*

#### 🎓 经济学家视角
**Focus**: Financial metrics, market dynamics, growth trajectories
- Analyze revenue growth, profit margins, cash flow
- Identify financial inflection points (break-even, profit surge)
- Evaluate competition 格局 (market share, pricing power)
- Assess AI/tech investment ROI timelines
- **Key question**: "What are the quantitative drivers of value creation?"

#### 📜 历史学家视角
**Focus**: Evolution patterns, historical parallels, strategic continuity
- Map company's transformation journey (e.g., "百团大战" → "本地生活霸主")
- Identify historical patterns (platform evolution, technology shifts)
- Compare with similar transitions (Alibaba: e-commerce → cloud; Tencent: social → industrial internet)
- Assess "era mission" evolution (information layer → logistics layer → intelligence layer)
- **Key question**: "What historical patterns does this transition follow?"

#### 👥 社会学家视角
**Focus**: Social impact, employment, governance, cultural shifts
- Analyze employment ecosystem (gig workers, SMEs, new job types)
- Evaluate城乡关系 impact (rural-urban service gap, agricultural supply chain)
- Assess governance role (food safety, algorithm transparency, data privacy)
- Examine consumption culture 塑造 (instant gratification, quality consumption)
- **Key question**: "How does this company function as social infrastructure?"

#### 💼 企业家视角
**Focus**: Strategic decisions, competitive positioning, risk management
- Review key strategic decisions (M&A, AI investment, market expansion)
- Analyze competitive strategy (differentiation, moat building)
- Evaluate financial strategy (loss tolerance, profit release timing)
- Assess risk response (price wars, policy compliance, tech uncertainty)
- **Key question**: "What strategic choices drive long-term value?"

## Pitfalls & Best Practices

### Common Pitfalls
- ❌ **Insufficient policy data**: Don't rely on outdated policy info; always search "近两周/近两月"
- ❌ **Superficial perspective analysis**: Each perspective must provide unique insights, not repeat others
- ❌ **Ignoring competition dynamics**: Always analyze competitive格局 (e.g., Meituan vs. Douyin 6:4 split)
- ❌ **Overlooking financial inflection points**: Identify break-even dates, profit surge timelines
- ❌ **Generic recommendations**: Tailor advice to user's risk profile (aggressive vs. conservative)

### Best Practices
- ✅ **Use structured tables**: For policy alignment, financial comparison, risk assessment
- ✅ **Quantify where possible**: Revenue growth %, market share %, profit margins
- ✅ **Provide timeframes**: 1-year, 2-year, 3-year predictions with specific milestones
- ✅ **Include catalysts**: What events will trigger value realization?
- ✅ **Offer portfolio strategies**: Suggest allocation ratios (e.g., 50% Meituan + 50% JD)

## Data Calibration & Fallback Strategies

### When Real-Time Data Is Unavailable
**Scenario**: Search tools cannot access future dates, specific time ranges, or return empty results

**Fallback Hierarchy**:
1. **User-Provided Data**: Always prioritize user-provided prices/metrics (e.g., "Meituan is 86.5 HKD")
2. **Historical Baseline**: Use last 1-3 months of data as reference point
3. **Industry Trend Extrapolation**: Apply sector-wide growth/contraction rates
4. **Relative Valuation**: Compare vs. peers, historical averages, or book value
5. **Explicit Uncertainty Flags**: Mark all estimates with "≈" and disclose limitations

**Example**:
```
Search fails for "XPeng 2026-04-19 price" → Use 2026-01-26 data (73.6 HKD) → 
Apply EV sector trend (+15% YTD) → Estimate 4月 price ≈ 85-95 HKD → 
Flag: "Estimate based on historical data + sector trend, verify with live market data"
```

### Model Execution Failure Handling
**Symptoms**: Task completes but generates "(No response)" or truncated output

**Root Cause Analysis Framework**:
| Layer | Possible Cause | Verification | Solution |
|-------|----------------|--------------|----------|
| **Data Layer** | Search returns no valid data | Check search results count | Add fallback queries, expand time window |
| **Model Layer** | Context limit exceeded, logic loop | Check output file size (<5KB suspicious) | Reduce context, add modular breakdown |
| **Prompt Layer** | Conflicting instructions, forced conclusions | Review prompt logic | Add "if no data, use historical trend" clause |
| **System Layer** | Output truncation, format errors | Check file size vs. expected | Add chunking, verify JSON structure |

**Prevention Strategies**:
- **Progressive Disclosure**: Show preliminary findings before final conclusions
- **Modular Analysis**: Break complex tasks into independent components
- **Cross-Validation**: Use multiple data sources for key assumptions
- **Output Verification**: After each run, check file exists, contains all sections, >2000 chars

## AI Industry Daily Analysis Workflow
**Trigger**: User requests daily AI industry news analysis with multi-perspective insights

**Step 1: Data Collection**
```
Search queries:
- "AI 人工智能 企业新闻 YYYY年MM月DD日 最新 头条 热度"
- "AI news today YYYY-MM-DD top trending tech companies"
- "artificial intelligence breaking news [date]"
- "AI startup funding acquisition YYYY-MM-DD"

Priority sources:
- Chinese: 36Kr, LatePost, 虎嗅, 界面新闻, 财新
- English: TechCrunch, The Information, Reuters, Bloomberg, Forbes
- Vertical: AI News, The AI Track, LLM-Stats, Hugging Face Blog
```

**Step 2: News Filtering**
- Time window: Last 24 hours (strict)
- Minimum 10 items (5 domestic + 5 international)
- Heat calculation: 阅读量×0.3 + 转载量×0.3 + 社交讨论×0.2 + 权威信源×0.2
- Exclude: Pure academic papers, non-enterprise news, outdated content
- Verify: Cross-reference 2+ sources per major story

**Step 3: Multi-Perspective Analysis**
Apply the four perspectives to each major story, then synthesize by time horizon:
- **Short-term (1-3 months)**: Event-driven, sentiment, catalysts
- **Medium-term (3-12 months)**: Earnings, commercialization, market share
- **Long-term (1-3 years)**: Industry structure, paradigm shifts, ecosystem

**Step 4: Output Structure**
```
【AI行业新闻深度分析】[Date]

Part 1: Top 10 News Summary
- Headline, Source, Time, Core Content, Heat Index (★)

Part 2: Multi-Perspective Analysis
- Economist: Macro/Industry/Financial implications
- Historian: Historical parallels/Evolution stage
- Sociologist: Social impact/Employment/Governance
- Entrepreneur: Business model/Competition/Execution

Part 3: Time-Horizon Investment Synthesis
- Short/Medium/Long-term opportunities

Part 4: Investment Ranking (by certainty)
- Tier 1 (★★★★★): Highest certainty
- Tier 2 (★★★★): High elasticity
- Tier 3 (★★★): High risk/high reward

Part 5: Optimization Suggestions
- Query prompt improvements
- Data source enhancements
- Analysis framework refinements
```

**Step 5: Continuous Improvement**
After each analysis:
- Review query effectiveness
- Assess perspective balance
- Validate predictions against outcomes
- Refine heat calculation methodology
- Update source authority rankings
